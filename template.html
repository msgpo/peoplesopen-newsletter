What's in this newsletter

The People's Open Network enables anyone to share their Internet connection or extend signal from neighboring nodes. Learn more at PeoplesOpen.net.

People's Open Network operates out of Oakland creative community and hackerspace Sudo Room. Sudo Room is a member collective of the Omni Commons. The Omni Commons is a 100% volunteer-run space for community organizing, collaboration, and creative production located in North Oakland. Learn more at OmniCommons.org.
